## Design Context

### Users
School directors, *sostenedores* (school network administrators), and decision-makers at Chilean educational institutions with 500+ students. They are evaluating Ethoz as a compliance and student safety solution. They browse on desktop during office hours, occasionally on mobile. Their job on this page: understand the product, feel trust, and schedule a demo. They are conservative buyers — trust and institutional credibility matter more than flash.

### Brand Personality
**Simple. Secure. Professional.**

Ethoz should feel like a competent institution — serious about data protection, approachable enough for a first-year teacher, and unmistakable when safety is at stake. The tone is **non-technical**: a school director should understand everything without Googling a term.

Emotional goals:
- **Primary:** "Trustworthy and they get me!" — This was built by people who understand the reality of running a school in Chile
- **Secondary:** Urgency — "The compliance deadline is approaching and we need to act"
- **Tertiary:** Relief — "Finally, a solution that handles the complexity for us"

### Aesthetic Direction

**Visual tone:** Cal.com modern-SaaS (2026-05-01 migration from McKinsey/medical-blue). White canvas (`#FFFFFF`), Cal black ink (`#111111`), light-gray feature cards (`#F5F5F5`), and dark Cal footer (`#101010`). Generous whitespace (96px section rhythm), 1px hairline borders, single primary action per band. Custom Cal Sans display typography paired with Inter for everything else. Light mode only above the footer — the dark footer is the deliberate single dark surface that closes every page.

**Structural references:**
- IntegratePIE.cl: Simple page structure, "¿Qué es?" and "¿Cómo contratar?" navigation, non-technical language
- Kimche.cl: Module-based product pages with "Productos" dropdown, WhatsApp-first sales, regulatory content as SEO
- Stripe / Linear / McKinsey publications: Restrained color, editorial hierarchy, information density without noise

**Anti-references (explicitly NOT this):**
- **Generic AI slop:** The #1 enemy. No cookie-cutter layouts, stock photography, identical card grids repeated 4+ times, or anything that looks like a template with the logo swapped
- Dark/inverted sections: No `bg-foreground` backgrounds, no dark trust bars or compliance sections
- Technical jargon: No "pgcrypto", "Row-Level Security", "pg_trgm" in user-facing copy. Keep technical terms for /compliance page only
- Data-dense dashboards: No Grafana/analytics feel
- Cartoon illustrations: No playful edtech startup vibes. This is institutional software for people with real responsibilities.
- Gradients, glows, warm accents, soft pastels: Pharma-clean is flat. One accent. Reserve it.

**Theme:** Light mode only. The audience manages schools during daytime. Dark mode is low priority — can be added later but is not a current need.

**Key rules:**
- **One accent color only.** Medical blue drives all interactive elements. Reserve it — a page with too many blue spots dilutes the signal.
- **Semantic colors only when meaning demands it** (destructive, warning, success)
- **NEVER hardcode colors** in templates — always use design tokens (`bg-primary`, `text-destructive`, etc.)
- **Warning pairs with navy foreground** (AAA 7.3:1). White on amber fails contrast — don't do it.
- Platform brand exceptions: `--brand-linkedin`, `--brand-facebook`, `--brand-instagram`, `--brand-youtube` — external identity, do not repurpose
- WhatsApp green `#25D366` is the only non-token color allowed (brand requirement)
- Selection: medical blue highlight at 25% opacity

### Typography (post 2026-05-01 Cal Sans migration)

- **Display family: Cal Sans** — `cal-sans` npm package (SIL OFL 1.1), self-hosted woff2. Used via `--font-heading` token for ALL h1/h2/h3 + `font-heading` class (125 uses across 30 files). Weight 600 (the only weight Cal Sans ships), letter-spacing -0.04em.
- **Body family: Inter Variable** — `--font-sans` token. Body, buttons, nav, captions, labels.
- **Numerics: JetBrains Mono Variable** — applied via `[data-numeric]` selector (numerics-only-tabular rule); inherits parent font in editorial contexts (Cal Sans for `font-heading` numerals, Inter elsewhere).
- **Playfair Display** — kept in fallback stack of `--font-heading` until Phase 7 cleanup (see `docs/superpowers/specs/2026-05-01-cal-com-redesign-design.md`).
- **OpenType features:** `cv02`, `cv03`, `cv04`, `cv11` on body for editorial feel.
- **Headlines:** weight 600 (never 500, never 700 — Cal authority is in the middle weight). Line-height 1.05–1.2 progressing by size.
- **Body:** regular (400), line-height 1.55, letter-spacing 0.
- **Hierarchy through weight and size**, not color. Cal Sans is monochrome — headings stay foreground (`#111111`), never primary-tinted.
- **Eyebrow (section tag):** `.eyebrow` or `.label-mono` — 12px uppercase, primary, 0.14em tracking. Editorial pattern (used in `/get-started`, `/productos`): `text-primary "Category" · text-border "·" · muted-foreground "meta"`.
- `text-wrap: balance` on headings, `text-wrap: pretty` on paragraphs.
- All font sizes via Tailwind scale — never hardcoded px values.
- Lint: `scripts/lint-cal-sans-scope.sh` blocks inline `font-family: 'Cal Sans'` overrides — Cal Sans is wired through `--font-heading` only.

### Spacing & Layout

- **Max content width:** `max-w-7xl` (80rem) for page containers
- **Section padding:** `py-16 sm:py-20` for major sections, `pt-28 sm:pt-32` for first section after NavBar
- **Grid gaps:** `gap-6` for card grids, `gap-8` for step layouts
- **Mobile-first:** Design for 375px, add `md:` and `lg:` breakpoints
- **Consistent alignment:** NavBar, content, footer all align to same `max-w-7xl` grid
- All spacing via Tailwind scale — never hardcoded values

### Radii — sharp, not soft

Radii cap at 8px. No oversized curves — this is a pharma-clean system, not a playful one.

| Token | Value | Use |
|-------|-------|-----|
| `rounded-sm` | 2px | Badges, tight pill markers |
| `rounded-md` | 4px | Inputs, selects, small buttons |
| `rounded-lg` | 8px | Standard buttons, nav pills |
| `rounded-xl` | 8px | Cards (capped at lg) |
| `rounded-full` | — | Avatars, status dots, pill CTAs |

### Shadows — hairline discipline

| Utility | When |
|---------|------|
| `shadow-card` | Default card lift (hairline) |
| `shadow-card-hover` | Hover state on cards |
| `ring-card` | Inset elevation for surfaces that need a subtle outline |
| `shadow-popover` | Dialogs, dropdowns |
| `shadow-glow-primary` | **Neutralized — no-op.** Utility retained for zero-file churn |
| `shadow-glow-destructive` | **RETAINED — safety signature.** Panic button, critical confirmations only |

### Components

- **NavBar:** Shared `NavBar.svelte` — fixed, `bg-background/80 backdrop-blur-lg`, Ethoz logo + Productos dropdown + locale toggle + CTA
- **Footer:** Shared `Footer.svelte` — 6 columns: Brand+Social, Producto, Recursos, Empresa, Para ti. NOT dark. NOT corporate.
- **Cards:** `rounded-xl border border-border bg-card` — icon + title ALWAYS INLINE (same row), NEVER stacked vertically. This is a hard rule — treat like an ESLint error. No icon-in-colored-box wrappers (AI slop pattern); use bare inline icons instead.
- **Buttons:** shadcn-svelte `Button`. Primary = filled medical blue. XL for hero CTAs. Elevation on hover comes from `shadow-card-hover`, not glow.
- **Icons:** Lucide via `@lucide/svelte` — size-4 to size-6. Default icon color: `text-primary` for action icons, `text-muted-foreground` for passive metadata.

### Content Principles

- **Non-technical language:** Write for a school director, not a developer. No database terms in user-facing copy.
- **Spanish-first, English-second.** All text via `t()` function — NEVER hardcoded in templates.
- **URLs in English** (`/pricing`, `/schedule`, `/features/safe-pickups`), content in both ES/EN.
- **McKinsey + pharma-clean tone:** Precise, authoritative, institutional. Every word earns its place. No buzzwords, no fluff.
- **Show the model, not the price.** Consultation-driven sales, WhatsApp-first conversion.

1. Landing page → understand the product (condensed features, "Conocer más" links)

3. Audience pages → role-specific value (/para-sostenedores, /para-directores, /para-porteros)

### Accessibility

- **Target:** WCAG AAA
- **Contrast:** Minimum 7:1 for normal text, 4.5:1 for large text — tokens are tuned to hit this on paper-blue
- **Motion:** Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` (global rule in `app.css`)
- **Color blindness:** Never use color alone — always icons + text + color
- **Keyboard:** All interactive elements focusable, visible focus ring (global `:focus-visible` safety net in `app.css`)
- **Touch targets:** Minimum 44×44px on mobile
- **Warning surfaces:** Navy text on amber — white on amber fails AAA, never use it

### Design Principles

1. **Trust through restraint** — Every element earns its place. White space IS the design.
2. **One color, maximum impact** — Medical blue for all interactive elements, one per screen. Semantic colors only when meaning demands it.
3. **Non-technical by default** — If a school director can't understand it in 5 seconds, rewrite it.
4. **Nothing hardcoded** — All colors, spacing, and text flow through design tokens and i18n. Easy to edit later.
5. **Flat, light, clean** — No dark sections, no gradients, no glow effects outside the destructive safety signature. `bg-secondary` or paper-blue for alternation.
6. **Compact, not corporate** — Footer, nav, and UI elements should be efficient, not sprawling. Less padding > more padding.
7. **Accessible by default** — WCAG AAA. High contrast, keyboard navigable, screen reader compatible.
8. **Unified with the app** — Landing → `/admin` → Ethoz-app should feel like one continuous product. Never diverge tokens.
